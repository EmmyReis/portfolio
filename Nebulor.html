Nebulor

Hey guys, Recently, I dedicated myself to this project heavily inspired by the movie Men in Black. The primary focus was to enhance my skills in look development and lighting, crucial elements in creating the dark and intriguing atmosphere characteristic of the film. From scene composition to background detailing and lighting, every aspect was meticulously planned to capture the visual and emotional essence I felt while watching the movie and drawing inspiration from its unique designs. The project not only challenged my technical and artistic abilities but also deepened my understanding of the importance of aesthetics in crafting visual narratives. I hope you enjoy it and I'm excited to share more of the projects I've been working on smiley
